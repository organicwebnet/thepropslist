BETA RELEASE

Help Us Build the Perfect Props Management Tool

You're using our beta version! Your feedback and bug reports are invaluable in creating the theater props management system you need.

What is a Beta Release?

A beta release is a version of our software that's feature-complete but still being refined based on real-world usage. Think of it as a "preview" version where we're gathering feedback to make the final product even better.

Why Beta Matters

Your experience using The Props List beta helps us understand what works, what doesn't, and what features you need most. Every bug report, suggestion, and piece of feedback directly shapes the final product.

What's Available Now

Our beta includes all the core features for managing theater props:

  • Create and manage props inventory
  • Track prop locations and status
  • Generate packing lists and labels
  • Collaborate with your team
  • QR code scanning and management
  • Photo documentation
  • Delivery tracking
  • Show-specific organization

How to Report Bugs & Issues

Found something that's not working as expected? Here's how to help us fix it:

1. Document the Issue

When reporting a bug, please include:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What actually happened
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Your browser/device information
  • Screenshots if helpful

2. Submit Your Report

You can report issues in several ways:

  • Use the feedback form in the app (if available)
  • Email us directly at support@thepropslist.com
  • Contact us through our contact page

Pro Tip

The more detail you provide, the faster we can fix the issue! Even small bugs help us improve the overall experience for everyone.

Feature Requests & Suggestions

Have an idea for a new feature or improvement? We want to hear it! Beta users often have the best insights into what would make the tool more useful for real theater productions.

What We're Looking For

  • Workflow improvements
  • New features that would save time
  • Better ways to organize information
  • Integration ideas with other tools
  • Mobile app feature requests

What Happens Next?

Based on your feedback, we'll be making regular updates to improve stability, add features, and refine the user experience. The beta phase typically lasts 3-6 months, after which we'll release the stable version.

Our Commitment

  • Regular updates based on your feedback
  • Quick response to critical bugs
  • Transparent communication about changes
  • Free access to the stable version when released

Ready to Help Shape the Future?
