PROPIVE

- Your Digital Props Journal

What is PROPIVE?

PROPIVE is a simple Android app that lets you journal about your prop projects, logging your ideas, progress, and notes for individual prop makers. You can also manage project budgets and log your time spent to help you get better at estimating future projects. You can also organize project files with Google Drive integration. You can also log details of materials used and even manage 3D print projects, logging the filament used and the settings and location of any STL files on your Google Drive.

Key Features

  • Project Logging: Log your prop-making projects with detailed notes and progress tracking
  • Time Tracking: Track time spent on each project and task
  • Google Drive Integration: Organize your project files using Google Drive
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor project milestones and completion status
  • File Organization: Automatically organize project files in Google Drive
