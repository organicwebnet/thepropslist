Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

Last Updated: January 2025

PROPIVE is a free service by The Props List that helps prop makers log and track their projects with Google Drive integration.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional)

1.2 Project Data

  • Project names and descriptions
  • Project files and documents
  • Timestamps and progress tracking
  • Notes and comments

1.3 Google Drive Integration

  • Access to files you explicitly share with PROPIVE
  • File metadata (names, sizes, modification dates)
  • Folder structure for project organization

1.4 Usage Information

  • App usage patterns and features accessed
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address and general location data

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • Provide and maintain the PROPIVE service
  • Sync your project data with Google Drive
  • Enable project tracking and organization

2.2 Communication

  • Send service-related notifications
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Share important updates about the service

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Debug technical issues
  • Develop new functionality

3. Google Drive Integration

Important: PROPIVE integrates with Google Drive to help you organize your project files. We only access files you explicitly share with the app.

3.1 Permissions

  • Read access: To view and organize your project files
  • Write access: To create project folders and move files
  • Metadata access: To track file changes and updates

3.2 Data Handling

  • Files remain in your Google Drive account
  • We don't store copies of your files on our servers
  • You can revoke access at any time through Google settings

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Project metadata stored securely in Firebase
  • Files remain in your Google Drive account
  • Data encrypted in transit and at rest

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure API connections to Google services

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information or project data to third parties.

5.2 Limited Sharing

We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who help operate PROPIVE (under strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Rights and Controls

6.1 Access and Control

  • View and download your project data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Revoke Google Drive access

6.2 Data Portability

You can export your project data at any time. Since files are stored in your Google Drive, you maintain full control over them.

7. Data Retention

7.1 Account Data

  • Retained while your account is active
  • Deleted within 30 days of account closure
  • Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance

7.2 Google Drive Files

Files in your Google Drive remain there according to your Google account settings. We don't control the retention of files in your Google Drive.

8. Children's Privacy

PROPIVE is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or through the app. Continued use of PROPIVE after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your data includes:

  • Consent: For optional features and communications
  • Contract performance: To provide the PROPIVE service
  • Legitimate interests: To improve our service and prevent fraud

This privacy policy is effective as of January 2025 and applies to all users of PROPIVE by The Props List.
