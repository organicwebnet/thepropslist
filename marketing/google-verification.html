Google Verification

This page is used for Google Search Console verification and other Google services.

Verification Methods

HTML Meta Tag

Add this meta tag to your site's head section:

<meta name="google-site-verification" content="YOUR_VERIFICATION_CODE" />

HTML File Upload

Upload this file to your site's root directory:

google[YOUR_VERIFICATION_CODE].html

DNS TXT Record

Add this TXT record to your domain's DNS:

google-site-verification=YOUR_VERIFICATION_CODE

Verification status will be displayed here once configured.
